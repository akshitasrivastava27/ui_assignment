Chikankari: The Delicate Embroidery Art of Lucknow for Tourists


Chikankari, meaning "hand-done" in Hindi, is a captivating embroidery technique that originated in Lucknow, India. This exquisite art form adorns fabrics with intricate white threadwork, creating a timeless elegance. The fabrics used for Chikankari include muslin, cotton, and silk. This traditional embroidery technique creates a textured and graceful effect. The designs of Chikankari feature ... floral motifs, paisleys, geometric shapes, and other intricate patterns. As a result, Chikankari has gained worldwide popularity as a timeless art form. Chikankari Embroidery, with its deep roots in the rich cultural heritage of Lucknow, has grown in popularity over the years.
